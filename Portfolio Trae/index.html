About Me

UX Designer

I'm a UX designer with a passion for creating user-friendly digital experiences. I love working with tools like Figma, Canva, Unreal Engine, Miro, and Mural to bring ideas to life. My approach is grounded in methodologies like the Double Diamond process and agile workflows, ensuring that every project is both innovative and user-centered.

When I'm not designing, you'll most likely find me in the forest, basket full of mushrooms (hopefully) and heart close to nature.
